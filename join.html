Join us

Do you want to participate in the Fanzine? Welcome to the team! You just have to send us an email to 8vipzsize at gmail with the following information:

  • Your contact information (Nick or name and email)
  • Your portfolio (any page or social network that shows your art)
  • In which edition would you like to participate?

Also, if there are more videogames that interest you and are not yet proposed by the fanzine, include them to take your suggestion into account in future editions.
